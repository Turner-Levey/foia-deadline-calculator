Local-first public records worksheet

FOIA 20 Working Day Deadline Calculator

Estimate the federal FOIA 20-working-day response target from a request clock-start date, add tolling or unusual-circumstances time when relevant, and export a memo, CSV row, or calendar hold for your request tracker.

20 working days, weekends and holidays excluded

This worksheet estimates federal FOIA timing only. It does not decide whether a request is perfected, tolled, expedited, remanded, appealed on time, or governed by a state public-records deadline.
